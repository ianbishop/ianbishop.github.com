YellowAPI Almost One Year Old

Nearly a year ago, I wrote a gem wrapper for the Yellow Pages Yellow API.

I had mostly forgotten about it, since no issues have been filed against it and no changes have happened to the API itself.

But, I was happy to see when I check its RubyGems page that it has had over 1,000 downloads in the last year.

That sort of feedback (1K downloads and 0 issues?) definitely inspires me to keep on giving.

Hurray for open source!
