About

I studied Computer Science at UNB Fredericton with major in Theoretical Computing, minor in Mathematics.

I like to write Clojure, Ruby and Javascript. I’m lucky enough to get paid to do that at UserEvents, a real-time stream processing startup.

I used to program competitively, competing twice at the ICPC Eastern Regionals at RIT against the likes of Harvard and MIT.

Sometimes I give talks at the Fredericton Developer User Group and teach workshops to undergraduate students at CS Square.

You can contact me at bishop14 [at] gmail [dot] com.
